My most recent trip was to: Iceland

This is a map of Iceland, the locations with squares around them were my favorite places to visit.

This location was just incredible. I had never been to an area that was that beautiful but, also cold and desolate. Although I did not get to experience the northern lights, I did get to experience an Iceland snowstorm during which we encountered while we were driving. I have never experienced an avalanche as a real threat until that day.
