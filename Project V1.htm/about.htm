Why Babson?

I came to Babson College because I want to one day start my own business.

I am currently concentrating in technology, entrepreneurship, and design here. I plan to graduate May 2020 and hope to work in business strategy or product management.

This school has taught me so much about taking risks and thinking differently and I hope to apply this to my future roles.

My Interests

My interests include painting. I really enjoy painting images of nature. I also like going to see art in different museums and especially like modern art.

I am also very interested in social impact. On campus I have had the opportunity to be involved with social impact initiatives on campus. I have been on the executive board of the Net Impact club, worked in the sustainability office as an intern, and have assisted the Lewis Institute since my freshman year.Academically I have also focused on learning every side to making sustainable business and technological solutions that can help the world. At my time at Babson I have taken Olin classes and I have also studied at the San Francisco campus which has given me a much deeper understanding of what it takes to make good technological solutions that help people. Being at conferences and being at Olin has proved to me the importance of knowing your products users and learning from industry experts. Applying my knowledge and learning more about social entrepreneurship I feel could help me bring a new perspective to campus and a more useful entrepreneurial mindset to finding solutions for challenging global problems.
